About F'aaet

On September 1, 2018, F'aaet opened its doors to an all new restaurant with a menu heavily influenced by nature. We find inspiration in the nature around us and use the ingredients of the forest such as roots, bark and resin in the food we serve. All our meat is grilled over open fire. We are serious about sustainability and quality and therefore, our raw materials are carefully selected and most preferably local. We've taken the classic American-styled burger - however serving it with a twist.
